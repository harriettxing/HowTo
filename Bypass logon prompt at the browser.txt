Internet Explorer:
- click on setting on upper right
- go to "internet options"
- select "security" tab
- click on "Trusted Sites"
- click on "Sites"
- add "https://www.glc-md.com" to the list of websites.
- Close
- Click on "custom level"
- Scroll to bottom till you see "User Authentication"
- Select "Automatic logon with current user name and password"
- restart IE

Google Chrome:
- click on menu on the upper right corner
- go to "Settings"
- open "advanced"
- click on "Open Proxy Settings"
- select "security" tab
- click on "Trusted Sites"
- click on "Sites"
- add "https://www.glc-md.com" to the list of websites.
- Close
- Click on "custom level"
- Scroll to bottom till you see "User Authentication"
- Select "Automatic logon with current user name and password"
- restart Chrome

Mozilla Firefox:
- start Firefox
- open a new tab
- type in "about:config" in the address bar
- scroll down the list of parameters till you find "network.automatic-ntlm-auth.trusted-uris"
- right click on "network.automatic-ntlm-auth.trusted-uris" and select "Modify"
- at the popup "Enter string value", enter "https://www.glc-md.com"
- click "OK"
- restart Firefox
